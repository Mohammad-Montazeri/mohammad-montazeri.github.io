About Me

I am a researcher and engineer specializing in robotics, biomechanics, mechatronics, and AI. I received my bachelor's degree from my dear University of Tehran. I served as a research assistant in the Human and Robot Interaction Lab (TAARLAB), working on my bachelor's project titled kinematic modeling and redundancy resolution of bio-inspired hyper-redundant serial-parallel Gough-Stewart manipulators via deep-learning methods, under the supervision of Dr. Mehdi Tale Masouleh. I'm currently studying master's degree at Sharif University of Technology in Mechanical Engineering under supervision of Dr. Saeed Behzadipour. I have also a one year experience in tutoring robotics (mechanics, electronics, programming) in Sigma Robotics Educational Group.
